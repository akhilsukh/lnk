How to use LNK

URLs are important. However, they're often long, segmented, and hard to share or remember. A URL shortener takes your long, unwieldy URLs and provides you with a short customizable URL that reflects your brand and purpose.
For example, a URL shortener can take in a long Google Slides URL: https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/17FV7dMR6Qu-70DVJZxemO137-20W8m2lDYye-Lk29gU/edit#slide=id.p and provide you with this short and sharable URL: https://lnk.it/disc-12.
